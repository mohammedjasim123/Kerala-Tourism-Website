Kerala is situated in the southwestern end of the Indian subcontinent. Kerala has a long history of art and cultural heritage and foreign trade with other countries. The state with the highest literacy rate in India, is noted for its achievements in education, health, gender equality, social justice, law and order. In addition to these, the state has the lowest infant mortality rate in the country. Kerala lies between the Arabian Sea in the West and the Western Ghats (Sahyadris) in the East with an area of 38863 sq km. It is one of the five states in the linguistic-cultural area known as South India. The neighbouring states of Kerala are Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Mayyazhi (Mahe / Mahe) is a part of Pondicherry (Puducherry) and lies within Kerala. Though the Lakshadweeps in the Arabian Sea is part of the Union Territories, it has a close alliance with the linguistic and cultural heritage of Kerala.

God's Own Country

British actor and fashion designer Sadie Frost is among a host of celebrities to get charmed by the beauty of Kerala.

Pakistani writer Fatima Bhutto, the granddaughter of former Prime Minister Zulfikar Ali Bhutto, was another high profile visitor to the God's Own Country, a few months ago.

In November last year, Rolling Stones lead vocalist Mick Jagger's daughter Jade Jagger, a celebrated fashion designer, stayed in a houseboat in Alappuzha, besides visiting Kollam.

Indian origin British actor-singer Preeya Kalidas tweeted "Kerala is sooo beautiful".

Beckoning her fans, Frost said, "Its bin amazin. spice boat cruise on Kerala backwaters has bin 1 of the best experiences of my life (sic)."

"Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows II" actor Bill Nighy's daughter Mary Nighy, an actor and filmmaker, has also given the thumbs up to Kerala.

Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan who recently visited Kollam was in all praise for the natural beauty of the district. "It's a wonderful place to visit," he said. Shah Rukh Khan who had visited the state several times said he always had a wonderful time and thrilling moments in the state.

Frost, who runs fashion label Frost French, wrote: "It is so peaceful and relaxing."
